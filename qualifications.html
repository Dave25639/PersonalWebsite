Teamwork and Communication

Years of collaboration with friends, classmates, and sometimes difficult personalities have taught me the value of communication and teamwork. In areas where I am qualified, I like to take the lead and divide the workload in a way that plays to each group member's strengths. I am an excellent listener and will try to hear every members opinion before making an important decision, such as whether or not we should use one framework over another.

In situations where I am not the most knowledgable, I like to play the role of the advisor while working had to learn and reach a higher level of competence in whatever it is my group may be working on. Also, because I am seeing the problem from a not yet informed perspective, I always make sure to question the choices being made rather than blindly follow orders in order to make sure we are making the best choice and so that I can see the reasoning behind why each choice was made that way.
