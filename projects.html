ClipFinder

Skills:
Python, API, Parsing, LLM

Created a simple application to be used by video editor. Many YouTube editors use stock footage in their videos, but it can be difficult to find specific scenes that the editor has in mind.

The solution was a program that took a description of your ideal video, retrieved the top search results from YouTube, and sent the information retrieved to ChatGPT to analyse and decide which of the videos are best fit for editing usage.

All sorts of information was considered, including the tone of the comments section messages, like counter count, whether or not the video was copyright free, and view count, to name a few.
